Introduction to EMS Desktop Client Version 44

EMS is user-friendly room scheduling software designed to manage the meetings and events that take place in your facility. Standard features, which you use to create, manage, and report on events, include the following:

  • The Reservation Wizard room booking tool
  • A graphical reservation book
  • The Browser and month-at-a-glance Calendar Inquiry
  • Numerous daily, sales, and statistical reports
