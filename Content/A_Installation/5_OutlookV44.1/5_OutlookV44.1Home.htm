EMS for Microsoft® Outlook Add-In Installation Guide

This guide provides information on installing EMS for Microsoft Outlook Add-in. EMS for Outlook is an optional add-in that integrates the EMS room reservation process directly with Microsoft Outlook 2010/2013.

Warning: To upgrade to Update 17 of EMS for Outlook, you will need to uninstall your legacy version and re-install.
