Video Conferences Not Visually Grouped 

Reported Issue

Issue Resolution

Reservation Summary

Video conference bookings were not visually grouped so that Host bookings and child bookings were obvious. 

Modified functionality to group and visually separate these parent-child bookings.

 Video Conference Host Reservation Incorrectly Cancellable 

Reported Issue

Issue Resolution

Edit Reservation > Cancel Bookings

When editing a videoconference reservation, the Cancel Bookings button was incorrectly available for the host room.

Modified functionality to make the Cancel Bookings option unavailable for video conference reservations designated as "Host."
