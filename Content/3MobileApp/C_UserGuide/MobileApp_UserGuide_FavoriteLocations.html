Assign Favorite Locations

Assigning favorite locations streamlines the booking process and filters your location search results.

To assign a location as a favorite:

  1. Tap the main menu icon in the upper left corner of the Home screen.

  2. Tap Favorite Locations.

  3. Click the + symbol to add Favorites.

  4. The Find a Location screen will appear. Search for rooms by Location.

  5. Choose a location from the Search Results list. When you search for locations during the booking process, those that are in your favorites list will be listed first.
